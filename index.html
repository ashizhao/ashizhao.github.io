Deployment Map

Show information about this page ↓  

To view various parameters of the stations in the USArray deployment click one of the menu items on the top right side of the map. "Zoom-in" by left-clicking on the map. "Zoom-out" by right-clicking on the map. Station symbols are colored by status parameter type.

To view details on an individual station you can either click a station symbol on any map below or click the station name in the table beneath (if present). Both methods lead you to a detailed information page for each station.

Subset on values by clicking the legend item. It will turn blue to show that you are activating a filter. Click the same value to cancel the filter.

Note: Offline stations will show a black circle around triangle.

Click the 'Hide information about this page' link above to hide this information.

Active Stations

There are currently 280 stations returning data out of a total number of 295 active stations (15 stations not connecting in the last 4 hours).
